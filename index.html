UNCOVER MORE INSIGHTS APRIL 15-17 IN SAN ANTONIO AT THE ARGENTUM SENIOR LIVING EXECUTIVE CONFERENCE

 

Featured Conference Sessions

A Focus on Development, Investment, and the Economy
Is the Sector Still Recession Proof?
With investment firms and economists predicting the next recession for 2020, what will be the impact to the senior living market? What should operators be doing now to prepare? What opportunities may arise for those best prepared for a recession?
Moderator: Chuck Harry, Chief of Research and Analytics, NIC
Panelists: Chris Bird, EVP and Director of Rental, LCS; Kevin Tyler, Vice President, Business Development, Welltower, Inc.
Insights from the C-Suite: State of the Industry
Learn what opportunities await the senior living sector in the near and long term, as operators balance the need for margin against the mandates of achieving operational excellence, obtaining strategic scalability, and winning the talent wars.
Panelists: Chris Hollister, President & Co-founder, Pegasus Senior Living; Kai Hsiao, CEO, Eclipse Senior Living; Lynne Katzmann, President and Founder, Juniper Communities
Insights from the C-Suite: The REIT Landscape
While labor costs, an influx of private equity, and competition continue to put pressure on profits, increased demand and innovative joint ventures may create opportunities for growth. At the same time, REITs are looking at the managed care model and affordability -- and waiting for opportunities to take advantage of failed private equity deals. Learn how executives are navigating the REIT landscape.
Moderator: Scott Brinker, EVP & Chief Investment Officer, HCP; Chris Cummings, SVP Asset Management, Ventas; John Getchey, Chief Investment Officer, ReNew REIT; Eric Mendelsohn, President & CEO, National Health Investors
What's Happening with Senior Living Liability Insurance?
In an environment where premium rates vary by state, how can the regional operator mitigate these costs - and what are the larger organizations doing to better manage their risk? You'll hear lessons learned in this practical session designed to provide you with actionable steps to take to lower your liability regarding liability insurance.
Moderator: Michael Pokora, Managing Director, Willis Towers Watson
Panelists: Thomas Masiello, VP of Enterprise Risk Management, Benchmark; Stacey Meyer, Senior Corporate Counsel, Brookdale
Reining in the Industry's Three Biggest Cost Drivers 
Labor, food, and utilities are by far the three biggest cost drivers for senior living. This panel of experts will provide guidance on driving down some of these costs through partnering and the use of the latest technologies.
Moderator: Jennifer Nolan, VP, Procurement Strategy, Brookdale
Panelists: Jim Boccher, President and CEO, ServiceMaster DSI
